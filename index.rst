What is UCard?
--------------
UCard is a versatile card issued by UnitedHealthcare that combines multiple benefits into one. It serves as:

- A UnitedHealthcare Member ID – Used at doctor visits and healthcare providers.
- A Prepaid Benefits Card – Covers eligible healthcare expenses like prescriptions, OTC products, and select wellness items.
- A Payment Tool for Medical Expenses – Accepted at participating retailers, pharmacies, and healthcare providers.

**Example:** Mary, a UnitedHealthcare member, uses her UCard to pick up prescription medication at CVS and later purchases a blood pressure monitor using her OTC balance at Walmart.

Key Benefits of UCard
---------------------
- One Card for Everything – Acts as both a healthcare ID and a payment method.
- Easy Payments – Covers eligible medical services, prescriptions, and wellness items.
- Over-the-Counter (OTC) Shopping – Purchase approved OTC health products online or in stores.
- Simple Balance Tracking – Check available funds anytime via UCard Hub.

**Did you know?** Many members use UCard at participating grocery stores to purchase approved healthy foods. Check your plan to see if this benefit applies to you!

How Does UCard Work?
--------------------
1. Receive Your Card – If eligible, your UCard will be mailed to you after enrollment.
2. Activate Your UCard – Visit https://activate.uhc.com or call the number on the card.
3. Use Your UCard – Pay for medical services, buy OTC items, and access member discounts.
4. Check Your Balance – Log in to https://ucardhub.uhc.com for real-time balance updates.

Ways to Check Your Balance:
- Online: UCard Hub (ucardhub.uhc.com)
- Mobile App: UnitedHealthcare mobile app (iOS & Android)
- Phone: Call the number on your UCard

**Pro Tip:** Some stores print your remaining UCard balance on your receipt after a purchase.

Who Can Get a UCard?
--------------------
UCard is available to UnitedHealthcare members, including Medicare Advantage and Dual Special Needs Plan (D-SNP) participants.

Where is UCard available?
Nationwide across all 50 U.S. states and U.S. territories where UnitedHealthcare operates.

Eligibility Requirements:
- Must be a UnitedHealthcare member enrolled in an eligible plan (Medicare Advantage, D-SNP, or another qualifying policy).
- Must be a U.S. citizen or legal resident with an active UnitedHealthcare policy.
- Must meet age or disability requirements.
- Must reside in a covered service area where UCard benefits are offered.

**Tip:** Unsure if you qualify? Visit UCard Hub or call UnitedHealthcare support.

What is UCard Hub?
------------------
UCard Hub is an online portal where members can manage their UCard account, check balances, and shop for eligible OTC products.

Key Features of UCard Hub:
- View balances for medical, prescription, and OTC expenses.
- Check benefits and spending limits.
- Shop online for OTC health products.
- Find participating retailers and pharmacies.
- Download statements and transaction history.

**Pro Tip:** Use UCard Hub to see which OTC items are covered under your benefits.

**How to use UCard to pay bills?**  
- Medical visits, prescriptions, and lab tests are eligible.  
- Not valid for rent, utilities, or internet.

**Can I use UCard on Amazon?**  
Some plans allow it for OTC items. Check UCard Hub.

**What can I buy with UCard at Walmart?**  
- OTC items, healthy food (if eligible), wellness products, medical supplies.

**Can I use my UCard for gas or clothes?**  
No. It’s strictly for health-related purchases.
